title: Introduction to Federated Learning
description: A look at how federated learning enables training models on distributed data without compromising privacy.
---

Federated learning is a decentralized machine learning approach that enables training models on data without moving it to a central server.

## Key Benefits

- **Privacy:** Data stays on individual devices (phones, IoT devices).
- **Security:** Reduced risk of large-scale data breaches by avoiding centralized storage.
- **Latency:** Models are updated locally on the device.

## Why use Federated Learning?

Federated learning is ideal for applications where privacy is a top priority, such as:

- Healthcare: Training diagnostic models on data from multiple hospitals while keeping patient records local.
- Finance: Training fraud detection models without sharing customer transaction data between banks.
