---
title: "The Role of AI in Material Informatics"
description: "How machine learning is used to accelerate the discovery of new materials for batteries, solar cells, and beyond."
---

**Material Informatics** is a rapidly growing field that combines material science with data science and AI. By using machine learning to navigate the vast space of possible chemical combinations, researchers are discovering new materials at record speeds.

## Current Techniques

- **Property Prediction**: Training models to predict a material's properties (like conductivity or strength) based on its chemical composition.
- **Generative Design**: Using models to suggest new, never-before-seen materials that might have a desired set of properties.
- **Active Learning**: A loop where the AI suggests experiments, and the results are used to refine the model's predictions.

## Impact on Sustainability

Material informatics is crucial for the green energy transition, as it helps discover the more efficient battery chemistries and more durable solar cell materials that we need to reach net-zero goals.

## The Importance of Open Data

Progress in this field depends on large, open datasets like the **Materials Project**, which provides researchers with millions of calculated material properties to train their models.
