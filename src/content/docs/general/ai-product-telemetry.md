---
title: "AI Product Telemetry"
description: "The product-level signals that reveal whether an AI feature is actually helping users."
---

Telemetry for AI products goes beyond tokens and latency. It measures whether the feature changes user behavior in a positive way.

## Useful Product Signals

- Acceptance rate of suggestions
- Edit distance between draft and final output
- Time saved on repeated workflows
- Escalation or retry frequency
- User retention for AI-powered features

## Why Telemetry Matters

A model can be technically impressive while still failing the product test. Telemetry shows whether answers are trusted, whether users complete tasks faster, and whether the feature creates new friction.

## Product Truth

The best AI metrics connect system behavior to user outcomes. That is what separates a model demo from a valuable product capability.
