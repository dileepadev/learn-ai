---
title: AI in Basketball
description: How artificial intelligence is transforming basketball analytics and player performance.
---

Artificial Intelligence is revolutionizing basketball at every level, from professional franchises optimizing player performance to coaches making real-time tactical decisions.

## Player Tracking and Movement Analysis

Computer vision systems track every player and ball movement using multiple camera angles. Neural networks classify actions — cuts, screens, dribbles, shots — with high accuracy. This data reveals insights invisible to the eye: defensive positioning tendencies, offensive patterns, and fatigue effects that influence performance throughout games.

## Shot Prediction and Shot Quality

AI models predict the likelihood of shots based on defender distance, angle, previous shot history, and game situation. Expected possession value models quantify how different actions — passing, driving, shooting — affect win probability. Teams use these insights to design plays that maximize high-quality shot attempts.

## Player Evaluation and Talent Scouting

Machine learning combines traditional statistics with tracking data to create comprehensive player profiles. Models evaluate defensive impact through positioning and anticipation rather than just steals and blocks. Scouting systems identify underappreciated players whose contributions aren't reflected in standard metrics.

## Injury Prevention

AI monitors workload — minutes, sprint distance, explosive movements — alongside reported fatigue and historical injury data. Predictive models identify players at elevated injury risk, helping coaches manage minutes and practice intensity. Some systems use computer vision to detect movement patterns that predispose athletes to specific injuries.

## Game Strategy and Lineup Optimization

Simulation models evaluate thousands of potential lineups and play combinations. AI suggests matchup-specific strategies based on opponent tendencies and predicted effectiveness. Real-time analytics help coaches make in-game adjustments based on what's working and what isn't.

## Fan Engagement

AI generates automated game summaries, highlights, and statistical insights for broadcast and digital platforms. Computer vision creates player tracking visualizations that help fans understand the game more deeply. Personalized content recommendations keep fans engaged throughout the season.
