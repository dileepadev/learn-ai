---
title: Ethics of Synthetic Media and Deepfakes
description: Understanding the societal implications of AI-generated content.
---

Synthetic media, including deepfakes, refers to media content (images, audio, video) that has been generated or manipulated using AI techniques. While it offers creative possibilities, it also poses significant ethical risks.

## The Risks

- **Misinformation:** Creating fake videos of public figures to spread lies.
- **Identity Theft:** Impersonating individuals for fraudulent purposes.
- **Non-consensual Content:** Generating explicit content without the subject's permission.
- **Erosion of Trust:** Making it harder for society to distinguish between truth and fiction.

## Mitigation Strategies

- **Detection Tools:** Developing AI that can identify synthetic media.
- **Watermarking:** Embedding invisible markers in AI-generated files.
- **Legislation:** Creating laws to criminalize the malicious use of deepfakes.
- **Media Literacy:** Educating the public on how to critically evaluate digital content.
