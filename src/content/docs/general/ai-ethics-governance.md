As AI becomes more integrated into society, ensuring its ethical use is paramount.

## Key Pillars

- **Fairness**: Mitigating bias in AI models.
- **Transparency**: Making AI decision-making processes understandable.
- **Accountability**: Defining who is responsible for AI outcomes.
