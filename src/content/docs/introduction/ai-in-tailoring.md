---
title: AI in Tailoring
description: How artificial intelligence is preserving and advancing the art of custom clothing.
---

Artificial Intelligence is tailoring itself to the clothing industry, helping custom tailors create better fits, work more efficiently, and serve more clients.

## Body Scanning and Measurement

AI-powered body scanners use multiple cameras or depth sensors to create detailed 3D models of customers. Neural networks extract precise measurements — including curves and postures that traditional tape measurements miss. These scans capture information once only obtainable through years of tailoring experience, democratizing access to custom fits.

## Fit Prediction and Alteration Recommendations

Machine learning models trained on millions of garment measurements predict how a particular pattern will fit a specific body shape. The systems identify where adjustments are needed — sleeve length, shoulder slope, hip accommodation — and suggest alteration approaches. This helps tailors communicate fit recommendations to clients and anticipate potential fitting issues.

## Style Recommendation

AI systems analyze body proportions, personal style preferences, and occasion requirements to suggest garment styles, fabrics, and details. Natural language processing extracts preferences from consultation conversations. Collaborative filtering suggests options based on what similar customers have chosen.

## Pattern Generation and Modification

Generative AI can modify standard patterns based on individual measurements, creating custom templates without manual draping. Some systems generate novel pattern designs optimized for specific body types and style preferences. This reduces the time tailors spend on pattern work while improving first-fit success rates.

## Quality Control

Computer vision systems inspect finished garments for stitching quality, seam alignment, and button placement. AI models identify subtle defects that might indicate pattern issues or construction problems. This ensures consistent quality across all pieces, regardless of which tailor assembled them.
