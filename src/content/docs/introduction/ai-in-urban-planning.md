Cities are massive, complex systems. **AI in Urban Planning** uses real-time data to make cities more efficient, sustainable, and liveable.

## Key Use Cases

- **Traffic Management**: Analyzing camera and GPS data to adjust traffic signals in real-time, reducing congestion and emissions.
- **Predictive Policing (Controversial)**: Using historical data to predict crime hotspots, though this faces significant criticism for perpetuating systemic bias.
- **Disaster Response**: Simulating floods or earthquakes to design better evacuation routes and emergency response plans.

## The Digital Twin

Many cities are building "Digital Twins"—complete 3D virtual models that are updated in real-time. Architects can use these to test how a new building will affect wind patterns, sunlight, and traffic before breaking ground.
