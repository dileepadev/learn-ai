---
title: AI in Cheese Making
description: How artificial intelligence is optimizing cheese production and quality.
---

Artificial Intelligence is maturing in the cheese industry, helping makers perfect their craft and produce higher quality cheese at scale.

## Milk Quality and Selection

AI-powered sensors analyze milk composition in real-time — fat content, protein levels, somatic cell count — before processing begins. Machine learning models predict how milk from different sources will perform as specific cheese varieties, helping buyers select the best milk for their products.

## Curd and Whey Management

Cheese making involves precise control of temperature, acidity, and timing. AI systems monitor these parameters continuously, predicting optimal cut times for curds and drainage rates for whey. Neural networks trained on thousands of batches identify the subtle conditions that distinguish exceptional cheese from merely good.

## Aging and Cave Management

Proper aging (affinage) requires maintaining specific temperature and humidity conditions while monitoring cheese development. AI systems track environmental conditions and predict optimal aging duration for each wheel. Computer vision detects rind development and potential defects, alerting affineurs when intervention is needed.

## Flavor and Texture Prediction

Machine learning models correlate production variables with final product characteristics. AI predicts how changes in starter culture selection, pressing pressure, or salt application will affect flavor development and texture. This enables makers to target specific profiles consistently.

## Production Optimization

AI schedules production to balance equipment usage, labor availability, and market demand. Quality control systems identify defects early, reducing waste and rework. Supply chain algorithms optimize inventory of rennet, cultures, and packaging materials.
