The legal profession thrives on documents, and **AI in Legal Discovery** (e-Discovery) is making the analysis of millions of pages near-instantaneous.

## Core Applications

- **Predictive Coding**: Using machine learning to identify relevant documents in a lawsuit, far more accurately than keyword searches.
- **Contract Review**: AI can automatically flag non-standard clauses, missing signatures, or expiration dates in thousands of legal agreements.
- **Legal Research**: Tools like Casetext use LLMs to find the most relevant case law based on the context of a legal brief.

## Benefits for Access to Justice

By reducing the billable hours required for routine tasks, AI has the potential to make legal representation more affordable for individuals and small businesses.

## Ethical Concerns

The "black box" nature of AI decisions in legal settings raises concerns about due process. Ensuring that AI tools are used to *assist* rather than *replace* human judicial reasoning is a primary focus of legal regulators.
