Fine-tuning allows you to adapt a pre-trained model to specific tasks or domains.

## Common Methods

- **Full Fine-Tuning**: Updating all model parameters.
- **LoRA (Low-Rank Adaptation)**: Efficiently updating a small subset of parameters.
- **Instruction Tuning**: Training the model to follow specific instructions.
