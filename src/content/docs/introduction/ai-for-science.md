While LLMs capture the spotlight, **AI for Science** is quietly transforming the way we understand the physical world. By integrating physical laws with deep learning, researchers are accelerating the pace of discovery by orders of magnitude.

## Key Frontiers

### 1. Drug Discovery & Protein Folding

Models like AlphaFold have solved 50-year-old challenges in biology, predicting the 3D structures of proteins which is essential for developing new medicines.

### 2. Climate Modeling

AI can simulate complex atmospheric interactions much faster than traditional physics-based models, allowing for more accurate and localized climate predictions.

### 3. Material Science

Generative models are being used to "design" new materials with specific properties, such as more efficient battery electrolytes or stronger, lighter alloys for aerospace.

## The Role of GNNs

Graph Neural Networks (GNNs) are particularly important in this field, as they can represent molecules and crystal structures as graphs of atoms and bonds.
