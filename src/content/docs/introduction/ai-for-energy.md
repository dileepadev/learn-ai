As the world transitions to renewable energy, the electrical grid is becoming increasingly complex. **AI for Energy** provides the tools necessary to manage this complexity and ensure a reliable, efficient power supply.

## Key Applications

### 1. Demand Forecasting

AI models analyze historical usage patterns and weather data to predict electricity demand with high precision, allowing utilities to optimize power generation.

### 2. Renewable Integration

Since solar and wind are intermittent, AI helps forecast when these sources will be active and manages the storage or distribution of the energy they produce.

### 3. Predictive Maintenance

By analyzing sensor data from transformers and power lines, AI can identify potential failures before they occur, reducing downtime and maintenance costs.

## The Future of the Grid

AI-driven "virtual power plants" are emerging, where millions of distributed energy resources (like home batteries and electric vehicles) are coordinated by an AI to act as a single, flexible power source.
