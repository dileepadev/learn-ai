---
title: "Enterprise AI Readiness"
description: "What organizations need before AI pilots can become reliable production systems."
---

Many organizations can prototype AI quickly, but far fewer are ready to deploy it responsibly at scale. Enterprise AI readiness is about the systems, policies, and operating habits that make production use realistic.

## Readiness Ingredients

- Clear data ownership and access rules
- Security review for models, tools, and vendors
- Evaluation processes for quality and safety
- Change management for prompts and model versions
- Business workflows that can absorb AI output responsibly

## Common Gap

The hardest part is rarely model access. It is operational maturity: knowing where the data comes from, who approves changes, and how failures will be handled.

## A Better Question

Instead of asking, "Can we build an AI feature?" enterprises should ask, "Can we operate it safely and improve it continuously?"
