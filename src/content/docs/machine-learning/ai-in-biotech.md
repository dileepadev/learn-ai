The same principles used to generate text are now being applied to the "language of life." **AI in Biotech** is rapidly accelerating synthetic biology and drug development.

## Protein Design (De Novo)

Instead of searching for useful proteins in nature, AI models like **ProteinMPNN** and **RFdiffusion** allow scientists to "design" proteins from scratch for specific goals, such as breaking down plastic or attacking a specific virus.

## Generative DNA Sequences

Models are being trained on genomic data to predict how changes in a DNA sequence will affect the behavior of an organism. This enables the engineering of:

- **Bio-fortified crops** that are more resistant to climate change.
- **Synthetic enzymes** for industrial manufacturing.
- **Targeted gene therapies** for rare genetic disorders.

## Ethical Boundaries

As we gain the ability to engineer biological systems with high precision, the conversation around biosecurity and the ethical limits of genetic modification becomes more urgent than ever.
